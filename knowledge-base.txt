ABOUT PRODUCT SPACE
--------------------
Product Space @ UC San Diego is the UCSD chapter of Product Space, a
nationwide community of students passionate about product marketing,
management, and design. The mission is to cultivate a tight-knit community
of product leaders who are well-prepared to guide impactful decision-making
in industry. Core values: Community, Empathy, Growth.

THE FELLOWSHIP PROGRAM
------------------------
The fellowship is a two-quarter mentorship program:
- WINTER QUARTER: Fellows complete weekly workshops hosted by experienced
  board members, covering three verticals — Product Design, Product
  Marketing, and Product Management.
- SPRING QUARTER: Fellows apply what they learned in a Capstone Project,
  showcasing their experience with the interactions between product
  marketing, management, and design.

OTHER WAYS TO GET INVOLVED
----------------------------
Participating in the fellowship isn't the only way to learn more about product!

Product Space strives to be a welcoming and supportive space by hosting
exploratory workshops, Q&A panels with professionals in product, and
networking events throughout the school year for any student interested
in product — not only fellowship applicants.

RECRUITMENT FAQ
------------------
Q: When does recruitment start?
A: Recruitment begins Week 6 of every Fall quarter. Exact dates, times,
   and locations are posted on the Product Space Instagram
   (@productspaceatucsd) — check there for the most current info.

Q: What is the best way to prepare for recruitment?
A: Getting involved early is the best way to prepare. Come to the
   exploratory/info events during recruitment week, follow the Instagram
   for updates, and fill out the Interest Form to stay in the loop.

Q: Do I need prior experience to apply?
A: No! We look for an eagerness to learn, teamwork, and initiative over
   prior product knowledge.

Q: Do I need to be a specific major for any of the verticals
   (Design / Marketing / Management)?
A: Not at all! Students from all majors are encouraged to apply.

Q: What is the time commitment as a fellow?
A: Expect around 6 hours per week of meetings and project work.

Q: Are there other ways to get involved besides the fellowship?
A: Yes. Product Space hosts exploratory workshops, Q&A panels with product
   professionals, and networking events throughout the school year for any
   student interested in product.
